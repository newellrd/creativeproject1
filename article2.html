Citizens of Punxsutawney, PA Are Met With Surprise This Groundhog's Day

The members of the community know the drill every Groudhog's Day: wake up at 6am to find out their future. Will Winter continue for six more dismal weeks, or can they start pulling out their sandals? Unfortunately, this year things didn't exactly go as expected when Punxsutawney Phil unexpectedly died.

Today was meant to be a happy day in history, but a death has caused tragedy that is striking the nation. Punxsutawney Phil, the nation’s elite groundhog, fell dead this morning of an apparent obesity related disease. Citizens watched horror struck as he took his last few breaths. “Without Phil, how will be know that weather awaits us this year?” one woman asked local reporters. To answer this question, scientist are trying to recreate the shadow by using a complicated mix of science, math, and astrology. News will be updated as soon as information is available, and funeral services for Phil will be held on Friday in the local prairies field. Thousands are expected to attend, including Taylor Swift, Eminem, and close friend Hugh Jackman.
